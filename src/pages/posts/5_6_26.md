Hello! Welcome to MMDungeon, my personal heaven on the vast World Wide Web. I wanted to create a section on my website that allowed for me to express my thoughts formally, like a collection of essays.

For this first entry I will just go more in depth about the purpose of this website, as well as any extra information I think is important. This will be short. I suggest reading my about page, as it provides surface level information on who I am and what I do.

The dungeon is a living space for my creations. All of my art, music, and games go here as an archive. I upload everything I finish here and on my other accounts. *My work will be only on the platforms I have linked on this website.* 

I do not condone the use of AI, training, reposting, and reusing my work by anyone.  I am a completely independent artist, and this helps me keep things organized for my own sake. It allows me to be comfortable with sharing my work on the internet, while staying true to my vision of what I want.

Anyone can reach out to me though my email or social media below. Maybe even leave a cute message on my guestbook.

Thank you.
